# Kechow — User Guide (Client / End User)

**Version:** v2 Analysis  
**Audience:** End users (customers) who order food via the platform  
**Note:** The application interface is **Spanish-first**; labels and screens are primarily in Spanish.

---

## 1. What is Kechow?

Kechow is a food delivery platform for Jiménez, Chihuahua. As a **customer**, you can:

- Browse restaurants and their menus  
- Add items to a cart and place orders  
- Track your order status  
- Manage delivery addresses and view order history  
- Leave reviews  

---

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Creating an Account

1. Open the app and go to **Registro** (Register).  
2. Enter your **name**, **email**, and **password** (with confirmation).  
3. Choose the role **Cliente** (customer).  
4. Submit the form; you will be logged in and taken to the customer area.

### 2.2 Logging In

1. Go to **Iniciar sesión** (Login).  
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.  
3. After login you are redirected to the customer home (e.g. Restaurantes or home).

---

## 3. Main Navigation (Spanish)

Once logged in as a customer, the top navigation typically includes:

- **Restaurantes** — Browse restaurants  
- **Mi Carrito** — View and edit your cart (with item count and subtotal)  
- **Mis Pedidos** — Order history  
- **Reseñas** — Reviews  
- **Perfil** — Profile and settings  

Navigation is role-based; only customer options are shown when you are a customer.

---

## 4. Browsing and Ordering

### 4.1 Choosing a Restaurant

1. Go to **Restaurantes**.  
2. Use the list (and any search/filters if available) to find a restaurant.  
3. Open a restaurant to see its **menu** (name, description, price, image).

### 4.2 Adding Items to the Cart

1. On the restaurant’s menu, choose items and quantities.  
2. Add them to **Mi Carrito**.  
3. The cart is **per restaurant**: if you add items from another restaurant, you may need to complete or clear the current cart first.  
4. Cart expires after a period of inactivity (e.g. 30 minutes); you may need to confirm or refresh before checkout.

### 4.3 Checkout and Address

1. Open **Mi Carrito** and review items and total.  
2. Go to **Checkout** (or equivalent).  
3. Select or add a **delivery address** (street, city, state, zip, etc.).  
4. Add any **delivery notes** (e.g. gate code, instructions).  
5. Confirm the order.  
6. After placing the order you can see it in **Mis Pedidos** and, if available, use **tracking** to see status.

---

## 5. Orders and Tracking

- **Mis Pedidos** shows your order history.  
- Each order shows status (e.g. pending, accepted, preparing, ready, out for delivery, delivered, cancelled).  
- If **Order Tracking** is available, you can open an order and see a tracking view (e.g. map or status steps).  
- You can **cancel** an order only in early stages (e.g. pending or accepted), depending on platform rules.

---

## 6. Profile and Addresses

- **Perfil** lets you manage your account.  
- You can add, edit, and delete **delivery addresses** and set a default.  
- Addresses are used at checkout for delivery.

---

## 7. Reviews

- **Reseñas** lets you view and manage your reviews.  
- You can rate and review orders/restaurants after delivery, if the feature is enabled.

---

## 8. Language and Accessibility

- The app is **Spanish-first** (locale `es`).  
- Skip link “Saltar al contenido principal” is present for accessibility.  
- If you encounter issues (e.g. wrong total, missing order), contact support or the restaurant.

---

All labels and flows in the app are in **Spanish** unless the product offers another language.
